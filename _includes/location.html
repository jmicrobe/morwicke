The ceremony and reception will be held at the historic Thornewood Castle in Lakewood, WA. The Thornewood is a nearly 500-year old Elizabethan manor, originally located in England but painstakingly shipped and re-assembled in Washington under the purchase of Mr. Chester Thornewood. More recently, the Thornewood was used as the filming location for the Stephen King miniseries Rose Red.
